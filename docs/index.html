Intro to R: A Hands on Tutorial

This book contains the materials for Intro to R: A hands-on tutorial, a multi-class R training initially createdd by the Urban Institute R Users Group and now presented to HUD staff by Ajjit Narayanan

Core lessons include:

  1. Data visualization

  2. Data munging (analysis)

  3. Getting data into and out of R

  4. Iteration

  5. Putting everything together
